Our Mission

Our mission is to combat climate anxiety by empowering youth. This webpage highlights initiatives in the Waterloo community, offering information on local green spaces, existing environmental activities, and opportunities for involvement. We aim to equip youth with a toolkit to support them in taking proactive steps to preserve their environment. We hope to inspire the next generation to choose a path toward a more sustainable future.

Navigating Eco Anxiety Together: Understanding Its Impact in Our Community

What is Eco Anxiety?

Climate anxiety is the constant worry and stress associated with climate change and its potential consequences for the planet and future generations. This anxiety often appearing in feelings of helplessness, sadness, and concern for the environment, which can contribute to broader mental health challenges. It reflects a growing awareness of environmental issues and their impacts, leading to emotional distress and a sense of urgency about the state of the world.
